Introduction & Purpose

This questionnaire has been designed by Queenston M&A Inc. to support a structured, efficient, and confidential sell-side mandate in the Canadian financial services industry. It covers every dimension a sophisticated buyer will assess during due diligence, while also helping us identify the ideal buyer profile for your practice.

What this document produces

  • Seller Psychometric & Strategic Profile — to match you with the right buyer
  • Confidential Information Memorandum (CIM)
  • Anonymous Teaser Document — 1–2 page summary
  • 10-Year Financial Projections Model
  • Transition Plan Summary

Instructions

Complete all sections. Fields marked ★ are essential for valuation. For financial data, provide the last five (5) fiscal years. Write 'N/A' where not applicable. All information is strictly confidential under our signed NDA.
